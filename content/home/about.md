# Home

I'm a Senior lecturer of soil hydrology at the Hebrew University of Jerusalem. In my lab, we study the interrelationships between soil physical, chemical, and biological properties and their influence on flow and transport at various scales. We combine observation, experiments and modeling to study complex soil processes. We are particulary interested in utilazing geo-electrical tools to image the subsurface.
